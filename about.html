About RDIY

Empowering Youths, Restoring Hope, Building Sustainable Communities

Restoration & Development Initiative for Youth (RDIY) is a youth-focused community development organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable young people through education, vocational training, rehabilitation, entrepreneurship, agribusiness, and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

Who We Are

A Community Driven Organization Creating Opportunities For Youths

Restoration & Development Initiative for Youth (RDIY) is a non-political, Charity driven community organization established to address the growing socio-economic challenges facing young people across Sierra Leone.

We work with vulnerable youths affected by unemployment, poverty, drug abuse, social exclusion, educational barriers, and limited access to sustainable economic opportunities.

Through practical empowerment programs and strategic partnerships, RDIY helps young people build skills, restore confidence, improve livelihoods, and become active contributors to national development.

Our Story

Why RDIY Was Established

Sierra Leone continues to face significant youth development challenges. Many young people struggle with unemployment, poverty, substance abuse, school dropout, mental health issues, and social exclusion.

National crises such as the Ebola outbreak, the 2017 mudslide disaster, economic instability, and the COVID-19 pandemic further worsened living conditions for thousands of vulnerable families and youths.

Recognizing the urgent need for sustainable solutions, RDIY was established to provide opportunities for rehabilitation, skills development, entrepreneurship, food security, and community transformation.

Today, the organization continues to restore dignity, confidence, and hope among young people while helping communities create long-term pathways to development.
