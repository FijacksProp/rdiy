About RDIY

Transforming Lives Through Youth Development

Restoration & Development Initiative for Youth (RDIY) is committed to empowering young people through vocational training, entrepreneurship, agribusiness, rehabilitation services and community development.

Our organization works closely with communities, government agencies, development partners, and private sector stakeholders to create lasting social and economic impact. By promoting inclusion, self-reliance, and community engagement, RDIY is fostering a generation of empowered youth who can drive positive change and support the development of stronger, more resilient communities.
