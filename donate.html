Why Donate

Your Support Creates Opportunities

Across Sierra Leone, many young people face unemployment, poverty, limited access to education, drug abuse, food insecurity, and social exclusion.

Through your support, RDIY can continue providing skills training, rehabilitation programs, entrepreneurship support, agricultural initiatives, and community development projects that transform lives.
